Visit Ancient Egypt

The Land of Pharaohs, Pyramids, and the Nile

Geography

Egypt is home to vast deserts and the fertile Nile River Valley. The Nile provided water, food, and transportation, making life possible in the desert.

Architecture

  • Pyramids of Giza — massive stone tombs for pharaohs.
  • Temples of Luxor and Karnak with giant columns and statues.
  • Valley of the Kings with richly decorated tombs.

Achievements

  • Hieroglyphic writing system.
  • Advances in farming and irrigation along the Nile.
  • A calendar based on astronomy.
  • Mummification and medical knowledge.
